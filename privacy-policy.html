Pixwik Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 27, 2025

Pixwik ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Pixwik website and mobile application (collectively, the "Platform"), an AI-powered ecosystem that bridges personalized learning, verified certifications, automated admissions, and hiring opportunities. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal data responsibly. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Platform. By accessing or using Pixwik, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Platform.

Note: We may update this Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification, and the updated Policy will be posted on the Platform with a revised effective date. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide, improve, and secure our services, including AI companions (e.g., Pi), the Gab layer for discussions and contributions, skill assessments, Gab Score calculations, admissions support, and job matching.

a. Information You Provide

  • Account and Profile Data: When you register, we collect your name, email, phone number, age, location (e.g., Tier 2/3 cities in India or global regions), educational background, learning preferences, and profile details (e.g., dynamic skill profiles).
  • Content and Interactions: Posts, comments, discussions, contributions (e.g., articles, tutorials), mentoring sessions, and feedback in the Gab layer.
  • Learning and Assessment Data: Responses to quizzes, assessments, simulations, projects, and AI interactions (e.g., learning style preferences like visual/auditory).
  • Admissions and Hiring Data: Application details (e.g., scores, budgets, documents for admissions), job preferences, résumés, and verified credentials.
  • Payment Information: For subscriptions (e.g., ₹199/month) or fees (e.g., ₹2,000 admissions success fee), we collect billing details via secure third-party processors (we do not store card info).

b. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Usage Data: Device type, IP address, browser, operating system, access times, pages viewed, and interactions (e.g., AI queries, Gab engagements).
  • Cookies and Tracking: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies for analytics, personalization, and ads. You can manage preferences via browser settings.
  • AI-Generated Data: Inferences from your activities, such as learning styles, Gab Score (based on content quality, skills, engagement), and match recommendations.
  • Location Data: Approximate location (from IP or device) for personalized content (e.g., vernacular languages like Hindi, Arabic).

c. Information from Third Parties

  • Integrations: Data from partnered institutions (e.g., seat matrices, scholarships) or employers (e.g., job postings) with your consent.
  • Social Logins: If you log in via Google/Facebook, we collect profile info (e.g., name, email).
  • Children: For users under 18 (e.g., ages 14–17), we require parental consent for data collection and limit features (e.g., no payment processing).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to operate and improve Pixwik, with your consent where required:

  • Service Delivery: Personalize AI companions (e.g., adaptive learning paths), calculate Gab Scores (content 40%, skills 30%, engagement 30%), automate admissions/job matches, and verify credentials via blockchain.
  • Analytics and Improvements: Analyze usage to refine AI (e.g., RLHF for agents), detect bias, and enhance features like Gab layer discussions.
  • Communications: Send notifications (e.g., score updates, job alerts) and marketing (opt-out available).
  • Security and Compliance: Prevent fraud (e.g., deepfake audits), enforce terms, and comply with laws (e.g., age verification for minors).
  • Research: Anonymized data for aggregate insights (e.g., skill trends), never identifying individuals.
  • Payments: Process transactions securely via third parties.

We do not sell your personal data. Gab Score is derived from your activities but remains under your control—simulators and breakdowns help you build it authentically.

3. How We Share Your Information

We share data only as necessary:

  • Service Providers: With trusted vendors (e.g., AWS for hosting, Cloudinary for media) bound by contracts to protect data.
  • Partners: Institutions/employers for admissions/hiring (e.g., sharing verified profiles with consent); anonymized analytics for improvements.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, subpoena, or to protect rights (e.g., fraud prevention).
  • Business Transfers: In mergers/acquisitions, data transfers with notice and consent.
  • With Your Consent: For features like Gab sharing or API integrations.

International transfers (e.g., to servers in US/EU) comply with GDPR/DPDP equivalents, using standard contractual clauses where required.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard measures:

  • Encryption (e.g., TLS for transit, AES for storage).
  • Access controls and audits (e.g., role-based for team).
  • Blockchain for credential integrity.
  • Regular vulnerability scans and bias audits for AI.

While no system is impenetrable, we commit to notifying you of breaches within 72 hours if required by law.

5. Your Privacy Rights

You have control over your data:

  • Access/Update: View/edit profile, Gab Score breakdown, or export data via app settings.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (we retain anonymized data for legal/compliance).
  • Opt-Out: From marketing, cookies, or data sharing (email privacy@pixwik.com).
  • Consent Withdrawal: For non-essential processing (e.g., personalized ads).
  • Children: Parental consent for under-18s; COPPA-compliant processes for US users.

For GDPR/DPDP rights (e.g., rectification, portability), contact us — we respond within 30 days.

6. Data Retention

We retain data as needed for services (e.g., profiles active until deletion) or legal reasons (e.g., 7 years for financial records). Inactive accounts decay scores and are deleted after 2 years.

7. Third-Party Links

Pixwik may link to external sites (e.g., partners); their privacy practices are not our responsibility—review them separately.
